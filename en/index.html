Property description

Modern, bright and ready from day one

Modern and bright country house located in Alcalá, in the heart of Colombia's Coffee Region, just minutes from PANACA and Parque del Café.

Built on a spacious 1,254 m² lot, this property offers functional spaces, excellent natural lighting and a direct connection with nature. It features an open kitchen, integrated living-dining room, study, two bedrooms, two full bathrooms, laundry room and an independent 12 m² storage room with potential for an additional bathroom.

Its 100% solar energy system significantly reduces monthly costs, and the satellite internet ensures ideal connectivity for remote work or vacation rental. The living perimeter fence provides complete privacy without losing the beauty of the natural surroundings.

Move-in ready or income-generating from day one. Furniture available optionally — ask the advisor.
